# Cassette Tape

A retro cassette tape–inspired Now Playing overlay with animated winding reels and classic tape aesthetics. Album art is displayed within the cassette's label area while the reels spin to evoke the feel of a tape actually playing — a small but satisfying detail that makes the overlay feel alive.

Perfect for streams with a nostalgic, vintage, or lo-fi vibe. Pairs especially well with synthwave, vaporwave, city pop, and any genre that leans into 80s or 90s aesthetics.

This overlay ships as part of the default Angels-NowPlaying bundle and is available to all users without any additional installation.

## Scene Recommendations

- Synthwave, retrowave, or vaporwave streams
- Lo-fi and chill music listening sessions
- Retro gaming streams, especially anything from the 80s or 90s era
- Stream layouts with warm, nostalgic colour palettes
