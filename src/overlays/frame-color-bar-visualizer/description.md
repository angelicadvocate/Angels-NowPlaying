# Color Bar Visualizer

An animated equalizer-bar style Now Playing overlay with a full-color spectrum display. Colorful frequency bars animate dynamically to simulate a live audio visualizer, making the overlay feel energetic and alive at all times even without real audio data feeding it.

Album artwork, track title, and artist name sit alongside the animated bars for a cohesive, music-forward presentation. Ideal for high-energy streams or any layout where you want a visually striking, kinetic music indicator.

This overlay ships as part of the default Angels-NowPlaying bundle and is available to all users without any additional installation.

## Scene Recommendations

- EDM, house, or high-energy music streams
- DJ streams or music production sessions
- Gaming streams where you want a vivid, colorful Now Playing display
- Scenes where motion and visual energy are core to the brand
