# Horizontal Classic

A compact, sleek horizontal Now Playing bar designed for minimal footprint. Smaller album artwork sits to the left of the track title, artist name, and a slim playback progress bar. The layout is purposefully understated — it tells your viewers exactly what's playing without pulling attention away from the main event.

This is one of the most universally useful overlays in the bundle. It tucks cleanly into the bottom of a gaming scene, sits as a lower-third, or functions as a persistent music indicator without ever feeling like it's in the way. Designed for a 728×180 OBS canvas.

This overlay ships as part of the default Angels-NowPlaying bundle and is available to all users without any additional installation.

## Recommended OBS Size

**Width:** 728px · **Height:** 180px

## Scene Recommendations

- Bottom-of-screen ticker bars during gameplay
- Lower-third placements where screen real estate is limited
- Streams where music is secondary to the main content
- Any layout where a clean, unobtrusive Now Playing indicator is needed
