# Vertical Panel

A vertically stacked Now Playing overlay with large, prominent album artwork sitting above the track title and artist name. Designed specifically for side panel and portrait-orientation placements — it fills vertical space elegantly without feeling stretched, padded, or out of proportion.

Where most overlays are built for landscape-orientation scenes, this one is the go-to choice whenever the widget needs to occupy a tall, narrow area: a sidebar next to gameplay, a panel beside a webcam, or a portrait scene.

This overlay ships as part of the default Angels-NowPlaying bundle and is available to all users without any additional installation.

## Scene Recommendations

- Side panel or sidebar placements on widescreen layouts
- Portrait-orientation scenes (phone streams, vertical TikTok-style content)
- Webcam-side companion panels
- Layouts where the Now Playing widget needs to fill vertical rather than horizontal space
