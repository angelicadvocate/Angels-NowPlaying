# Retro Vinyl

A classic vinyl record–inspired Now Playing overlay with a continuously spinning animated disc. Album artwork is displayed at the center of the record while it rotates at a steady RPM — a satisfying, timeless detail that evokes the simple pleasure of putting on a record and letting it play.

Warm in tone and nostalgic by design, this overlay pairs beautifully with jazz, soul, blues, classic rock, and any genre that benefits from an analogue, vinyl-era aesthetic. Also works well for lo-fi streams and chill ambient sessions.

This overlay ships as part of the default Angels-NowPlaying bundle and is available to all users without any additional installation.

## Scene Recommendations

- Jazz, soul, blues, or classic rock listening streams
- Lo-fi and chill ambient or study streams
- Vinyl collector or record-shopping content
- Any layout with warm tones and a nostalgic, analogue feel
