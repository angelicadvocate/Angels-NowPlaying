# Glassmorphism 1

A clean, modern Now Playing overlay built around a frosted glass aesthetic. Large album artwork is displayed behind a translucent, blurred panel that softens the background and gives the widget a polished, layered look. A slim playback progress bar sits below the track title and artist name.

The glassmorphism style adapts naturally to a wide variety of stream backgrounds without visually clashing, making it one of the most versatile overlays in the default bundle. It works equally well as a corner widget, a mid-scene element, or a focal point on a music-focused layout.

This overlay ships as part of the default Angels-NowPlaying bundle and is available to all users without any additional installation.

## Scene Recommendations

- Modern or minimal stream aesthetics
- Gaming streams with dark or gradient backgrounds
- Streams wanting a clean, premium-looking overlay
- Corner placements or mid-scene Now Playing widgets
