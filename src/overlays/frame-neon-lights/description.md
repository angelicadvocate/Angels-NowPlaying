# Neon Lights

A high-energy Now Playing overlay with vivid glowing neon borders, animated light effects, and vibrant accent colors on a deep dark background. The neon glow pulses subtly to keep the overlay feeling alive between song updates, and the overall aesthetic leans hard into cyberpunk, vaporwave, and electric nightlife territory.

One of the most eye-catching overlays in the default bundle — it makes the biggest visual impact and works best when your stream's identity already embraces bright colors, glowing UI elements, and a tech-forward dark aesthetic.

This overlay ships as part of the default Angels-NowPlaying bundle and is available to all users without any additional installation.
