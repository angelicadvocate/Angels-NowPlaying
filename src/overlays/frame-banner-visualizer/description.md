# Banner Visualizer

A fullscreen banner-style Now Playing overlay featuring animated pseudo-visualizer bars. Designed for high-impact moments when you want the music to take center stage — full-scene breaks, lofi or ambient streams, or any layout where you want something visually bold without sacrificing clarity.

The visualizer bars animate continuously to simulate a live audio spectrum, keeping the overlay feeling alive even between song updates. Album artwork is displayed prominently alongside the track title and artist name for an immersive music-forward presentation.

This overlay ships as part of the default Angels-NowPlaying bundle and is available to all users without any additional installation.

## Scene Recommendations

- Full-scene "Now Playing" breaks between gameplay segments
- Lo-fi, ambient, or long-form music streams
- Countdown or BRB screens where the music is the main focus
- Layouts where energy and visual presence are part of the brand
